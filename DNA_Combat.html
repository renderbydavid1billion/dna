DEAD & AWAKE

  • Combat

Who goes first
  Initiative is determined by Evade

Action
  You get 2 actions on your turn : Attacking, Movement, Using Items, Communicating and Environment interaction  

Attacking
1. Aiming
  Choose where to aim and roll to hit. You must match or beat the difficulty set by DM
          Head = 3 Successes (Does double damage)
          Hands = 2 Successes (Knocks items out of hands)
          Feet = 2 Successes (Prevents enemy from moving on their next turn)
          Torso = 1 Success

  Add a bonus to your roll(s) from your skills, either Range or Melee, + your Tactics

2. Evade
  When you match the difficulty with all dice in your roll, the enemy cannot evade you
  If you don't match but still succeed
       > Defender tries to evade by rolling 1D20. They must beat your highest roll to evade your attack

       > If they match your highest roll with a natural, they can try to counter attack you
         To counter attack, they must roll 1D20 to beat your highest roll with a natural

3. Dealing Damage
  If the Defender is wearing armor that is effective in blocking your attack type, take the armor rating and subtract it from    the damage your weapon deals

  Other than that, you deal the amount of damage your weapon deals

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Movement
 Everyone has at least 1 movement which is equivalent to 1 grid space
 If you have points in Athletics you may add them to your movement

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Using Items
 You may choose to interact with an item in your inventory

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Communicating
 As an action you can use your Social skill set to communicate with a defender(s) or party member(s)
 Whoever you choose to communicate with must be at least within 5 tiles of your position

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Environment Interaction
 Within 1 tile of an environmental object, you may interact with it
